Delete Your Data

Underdial has no account. Your collection lives on your device, so you control deletion entirely.

Option 1 — Reset From Inside the App (recommended)

  1. Open Underdial on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Reset Data.
  4. Confirm by tapping Reset.

Your box, wrist settings, and check history are removed immediately.

Option 2 — Delete the App

Because nothing is stored on our servers, deleting Underdial from your iPhone removes all of its local data along with it. There is no separate server-side record to request the deletion of.

What gets deleted

  • Your box (every watch you added).
  • Your wrist size and fit preferences.
  • Your check history and any saved verdicts.
  • Your rotation tracking and Box Audit data.

What is retained

For legal and billing purposes the following may persist, none of it tied to your identity:

  • Anonymized purchase records for Underdial Plus (held by Apple).
  • Anonymized aggregate analytics that cannot be traced back to you.

Subscriptions

Deleting the app or resetting your data does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. You must cancel separately in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or it will continue to renew.
